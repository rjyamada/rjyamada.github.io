About Me

I'm an engineering leader, product manager, data analyst, developer, and entrepreneur. In the last decade, I've worked on various websites including FamilyFinds, CakeStyle, and Groupon. I leverage my startup and engineering experiences to drive projects from start to finish.

Currently, I manage a handful of software engineers with experiences ranging from 1 to 8 years. I work closely with my team to plan and execute projects by being involved in technical discussions, architecture reviews, and sprint prioritization meetings. As a product manager, I strive to put consumers' interests first and rely on data to make critical decisions. I enjoy thinking of creative ways to collect data and build valuable features for our customers.
